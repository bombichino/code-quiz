Coding Quiz Time

It's everyone's favorite, trivia! But without the bar, the snacks, and the sad prizes you wouldn't even spend your tickets on at Dave and Buster's to bring home.

Here are the rules:

  • Once you click start, a timer will begin. The whole scoring system of this quiz is based on the timer - so that's important!
  • For each question, pick one answer. There is only one correct answer. Otherwise, contact your quiz master and contest.You only have one attempt per question, then you move forward; it's about speed!
  • When you get a question right, you'll advance.
  • When you get a question wrong, 5 seconds will be deducted from your time.
  • The game is over when all the questions are answered or the time runs out.
  • Your final score is what is left on the clock.
